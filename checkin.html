Online check-in

Check in for your virtual flight

Use the booking reference you received when you booked. Our staff will confirm your check-in and share gate details on Discord.

• Check in using your booking reference & Roblox username

Find your booking

Enter your booking reference exactly as it appears (for example: EGR4K7M2), plus your Roblox and Discord details.

Booking

Staff will verify your eligibility for Premium Economy, Business and First against your booking & investor status.

Passenger details

For group bookings, check-in is handled by the lead passenger. Make sure the Roblox username matches one of the passengers on the booking.

Assistance & notes

ℹ Checking in does not guarantee boarding if booking details are invalid or requirements for premium cabins are not met.

What happens next

  • Your check-in request is posted to our Online Check-in channel on Discord.
  • A staff member will match it with your booking reference and cabin.
  • Once confirmed, they’ll send you gate / server details and any extra instructions.

Tips

  • Make sure your Roblox username is spelled exactly as in your booking.
  • Keep DMs open so staff can contact you.
  • Have your booking reference ready when joining the flight.
